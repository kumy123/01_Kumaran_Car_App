Do you want to make payment?

This is an interactive web Car Show Room application to simulate and manage the sale of cars in a store where there is 10 customers in a queue that you have to drag them one by one onto the correct car brand. After that, you will drop them into the cashier section to pay or drop them into the exit section.

How to play this game:

1. Customers arrive at the store are presented in a queue and the queue can only receive a maximum of 10 customers at any one time. Only the first customer in queue can be move, the others must wait for their turn.
2. The application will display a list of cars for sale in the store. The cars are grouped by brand on the sales floor: Porche with 4 cars, Volkswagen with 6 cars, Audi with 5 cars and BMW with 3 cars.
3. The application only allows customer that is being moved to occupy a car that matches the brand that they are interested in. If customer is dropped in other place, they will return to the top of the queue.
4. A car that is being used by a customer cannot be used by another until the original customer is moved to another or to exit, thus freeing the position.
5. There will be an exit area where customer can leave the store immediately and a cashier area where customers decide if they would like to buy the visited car or not.
